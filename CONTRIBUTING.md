# Contributing to CogMesh

Welcome, and thank you for considering a contribution to CogMesh! 🎉

## ⚠️ Read first: licensing of contributions

CogMesh is **dual-licensed** — free and open source under **AGPL-3.0-or-later**
(see [`LICENSE`](./LICENSE)), with a [commercial option](./COMMERCIAL-LICENSE.md).
Contributions are governed by the [**Contributor License Agreement (`CLA.md`)**](./CLA.md).

**By opening a pull request, submitting a patch, or otherwise contributing, you
agree to the CLA.** In short, that means:

- you **assign** (or, where assignment isn't possible, exclusively **license**) the
  rights in your contribution to the author, 심태양 (Shim Taeyang) — this is what lets
  CogMesh be offered under both the AGPL and a commercial license;
- your contribution becomes part of CogMesh under the same License — it does **not**
  create any separate ownership, co-authorship, or commercialization right for you;
  and
- you confirm the contribution is your own original work and that you have the right
  to submit it.

This keeps CogMesh's ownership clear so the project's protections stay intact for
everyone. If you can't agree to the CLA, please don't submit a contribution.

## How to contribute

1. **Discuss first** for anything non-trivial — open an issue describing the change.
2. **Fork & branch** from the default branch.
3. **Keep the license headers** at the top of each source file intact. New source
   files must include the same SPDX header (see any existing file for the template).
4. **Do not alter or remove** the provenance markers, the whitepaper coordinate
   values/formulae, or the notices in `README` and the whitepaper.
5. **Sign off** each commit with the Developer Certificate of Origin line:
   ```
   Signed-off-by: Your Name <your.email@example.com>
   ```
   This certifies your agreement to the CLA.
6. **Open a pull request** with a clear description of what and why.

welcome on that basis. Any commercial use — including of a contributed or modified
version — requires a commercial license from the author
(see COMMERCIAL-LICENSE.md). Reach out if that's what you need.

Thanks again for helping CogMesh grow! 🚀
